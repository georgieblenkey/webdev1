My Art

Portfolio Homepage

I use different mediums to create my art, from ink, to graphite, to acrylic paint. I use digital to traditional practices, and techniques like collage, to painting, to illustration, to printmaking.

I've been making art for as long as I can remember, starting with crayons, markers, watercolors, and more In elementary school, art class has always been my favorite class because of the materials I could work with.

I started making digital art around age 11 when I got my first Wacom tablet. Before that, I would make art on Microsoft Paint with my mouse. Now, I have a touchscreen tablet to make digital art even easier for me.

Today, I'm also starting my journey with oil painting and I'm beginning to learn the ways that technique needs to be different than using acrylics or watercolors. Hopefully, soon I can show off my oil paintings as well.
